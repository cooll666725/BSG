About — Black Sun Games

About Black Sun Games

Imagination engineered into play.

Founded by creators who believe that games should spark wonder, challenge the mind, and invite exploration, Black Sun Games Inc. is an independent game development studio dedicated to crafting worlds that feel alive — from the first line of code to the last flicker of light in a fantasy sky.

Originally established as a small VR-focused studio, Black Sun Games has since evolved into a versatile developer of PC and mobile experiences that combine depth, creativity, and accessibility. Our titles balance rich lore, stylized art, and solid design — blending classic inspirations with modern technology to create gameplay that’s as memorable as it is replayable.

At our core, we’re worldbuilders and storytellers.
We build universes that players can return to again and again — whether it’s the whimsical chaos of Max Twist, the high-stakes arena of Space Vault: Alien Coliseum, or the strategic empire building of Terrestrial Conquest, where board-game clarity meets 4X ambition.

Our Mission

To create games that ignite curiosity, reward creativity, and bring players together — across genres, platforms, and worlds. Every project we make aims to be more than a game; it’s an experience shaped by imagination, driven by design, and refined through play.

Our Philosophy

  • Player-First Design: Fun, fairness, and freedom of choice guide every mechanic.
  • Innovation with Heart: We experiment with systems that surprise without sacrificing clarity.
  • Sustainable Creativity: Independent development done with integrity, collaboration, and craft.

Black Sun Games Inc. is more than a studio — it’s a growing constellation of dreamers, engineers, and artists building universes worth exploring.

Meet the Team

Mad Scientist Drake

The Mad Scientist is the company's President and one of the main developers for UI and game development. He has created many potent potions to keep the crew running when nothing else works. He prays to the “Lord of Latte” and is a major reason the work gets done.

Admiral Starburns

The Admiral keeps us all in line when we have bad ideas and gives us ideas that are far better—but not always agreeable. He is the company Secretary and, with a little help from Drake, wrangles the social media. If you've seen anything on our X/Twitter or Facebook—blame him. He keeps the media flowing.

Flight Commander Penguin

Penguin is the almighty leader in all things Dev, Math, and Physics. He is the company Treasurer, and more than that, bends the laws at will (and breaks them with the stroke of his keys if they don't fit his needs). He can and will recreate gravity at every turn and ensures the complex systems are operating on all reactors. Nuclear.
