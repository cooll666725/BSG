About the Game

Max Twist Intro: In this futuristic Xtreme Sport, you'll mount hover boards and spiral down eye-popping sci-fi tracks full of obstacles. Smoke speed records and set seamless twist scores to a heart-pumping soundtrack. Inspired by games like 1080° Snowboarding and F-Zero, Max Twist takes the concept to the space age. Speed through adrenaline-pumping sci-fi courses as you dodge oncoming walls and obstacles in style. With both a Casual Circuit and Pro Circuit, you can play for both relaxation and to edge out you and your friends' best scores! But don't expect mercy from the track! Stay on your board, stay twisting, and show each course who's boss!

Details:

  • 18 official tracks
  • 10 thumping music tracks
  • Tutorial level and "Learn More" menu for beginners
  • Sci-fi themed spaceship HUB for selecting courses and music
  • VR touch-based menu controls
  • Compatible with the HTC Vive, HTC Cosmos, Oculus Rift, Oculus Quest, and Oculus Quest 2
